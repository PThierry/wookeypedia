Tataouine directories
---------------------

Tataouine contains the following main directories:

+------------+------------------------------------------+
| Directory  | Purpose                                  |
+============+==========================================+
| kernel     | the EwoK microkernel                     |
+------------+------------------------------------------+
| drivers    | Userspace drivers of various hardware IP |
+------------+------------------------------------------+
| libs       | Userspace libraries                      |
+------------+------------------------------------------+
| loader     | Platform bootloader                      |
+------------+------------------------------------------+
